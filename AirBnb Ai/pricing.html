Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes! You can try HostGenius for 14 days completely free. No credit card required during the trial period.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.

How does the M-Pesa integration work?

Our M-Pesa integration allows you to securely request and receive payments from guests directly through the platform, with automatic booking reconciliation.

Do you offer discounts for yearly billing?

Yes! When you choose annual billing, you save 20% compared to the monthly plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and M-Pesa for Kenyan customers.

Is my data secure?

Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we never store your payment details.

Still have questions?

Contact our support team who will be happy to help you choose the right plan for your needs.
